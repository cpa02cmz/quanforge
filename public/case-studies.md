## üí° **Lessons Learned & Best Practices**

### **Key Success Factors**

1. **Clear Strategy Definition**
   - Well-defined trading rules
   - Specific risk parameters
   - Clear performance objectives

2. **Proper Backtesting**
   - Sufficient historical data (10+ years)
   - Out-of-sample testing
   - Stress testing scenarios

3. **Risk Management**
   - Position sizing rules
   - Stop-loss mechanisms
   - Portfolio diversification

4. **Continuous Optimization**
   - Regular strategy review
   - Parameter tuning
   - Market adaptation

### **Common Pitfalls to Avoid**

1. **Over-optimization**
   - Curve fitting to historical data
   - Ignoring out-of-sample performance
   - Excessive parameter tuning

2. **Poor Risk Management**
   - Inadequate position sizing
   - Missing stop-loss mechanisms
   - Concentrated positions

3. **Market Regime Changes**
   - Ignoring market structure changes
   - Static strategy parameters
   - Lack of adaptation mechanisms
