## üìä Company Overview

### About QuantForge AI
QuantForge AI is a leading technology company revolutionizing the world of algorithmic trading through artificial intelligence. Our platform enables traders of all skill levels to create sophisticated MQL5 trading robots using natural language descriptions, making quantitative trading accessible to everyone.

### Key Facts
- **Founded**: 2023
- **Headquarters**: New York, NY (with offices in London and Singapore)
- **Users**: 50,000+ active traders
- **Trading Robots Created**: 1,000,000+
- **Funding**: $15M total (Seed + Series A)
- **Team Size**: 75+ employees
- **Markets**: Global presence in 100+ countries

### Mission & Vision
**Mission**: To democratize algorithmic trading by making sophisticated quantitative strategies accessible to everyone through the power of artificial intelligence.

**Vision**: A world where every trader has access to the tools and knowledge needed to succeed in automated trading, regardless of their technical background or financial resources.
