# QuantForge AI - Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is QuantForge AI?
QuantForge AI is an advanced web-based platform that uses Google's Gemini AI models to generate, analyze, and manage trading robots (Expert Advisors) for the MetaTrader 5 (MQL5) platform.

### How does QuantForge AI work?
Simply describe your trading strategy in natural language, and our AI will generate professional MQL5 code. You can then configure parameters, run simulations, and deploy to MetaTrader 5.

### Is QuantForge AI free to use?
Yes, QuantForge AI offers free access to generate and manage trading robots. You only need your own API keys for the AI service.

## Technical Requirements

### What do I need to use QuantForge AI?
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- MetaTrader 5 platform for deploying generated robots
- Optional: Google Gemini API key for AI generation

### Which trading platforms are supported?
Currently, QuantForge AI specializes in MetaTrader 5 (MT5) MQL5 Expert Advisors.

### Can I use the generated robots on any broker?
Yes, the generated MQL5 robots work with any broker that supports MetaTrader 5.

## Troubleshooting

### My code generation failed. What should I do?
